City Breaks Heat Record as Temperatures Soar

Officials Urge Residents to Stay Hydrated and Indoors

In an unprecedented turn of events, the city witnessed the hottest day on record, with temperatures climbing well above seasonal norms. Public health departments have issued advisories recommending limited outdoor activity during peak hours.

Meanwhile, local businesses are adapting to the heatwave by adjusting hours and providing cooling stations for customers. Meteorologists suggest the heat will persist through the weekend before a slight cooldown early next week.
